PARAMETRIC DESIGN BRIDAL MUSHROOM

Computational & Generative Design with Rhino Grasshopper

Project Overview

A collection of parametric designs exploring computational design principles and generative algorithms. This project demonstrates the intersection of mathematics, design, and digital fabrication through complex geometric structures.

Technologies Used

Rhino 3D Grasshopper Python KeyShot Parametric Design Generative Algorithms

Project Details

This parametric design project explores the possibilities of computational design through the creation of complex geometric forms. Using Rhino Grasshopper, I developed algorithms that generate organic structures based on mathematical principles and natural patterns.

The featured lamp design showcases how parametric modeling can create forms that would be impossible to achieve through traditional design methods. The structure is generated through a series of mathematical operations that create both aesthetic beauty and functional lighting distribution.

This project demonstrates proficiency in computational design thinking, algorithmic problem-solving, and the ability to bridge the gap between digital design and physical fabrication. The parametric approach allows for infinite variations while maintaining design integrity and structural viability.
