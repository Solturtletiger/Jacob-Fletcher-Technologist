WATER BOTTLE

Ergonomic Design & Form Development

Project Overview

An ergonomic water bottle design that prioritizes user comfort and functionality. This project explores human-centered design principles while addressing sustainability and manufacturing considerations in consumer product development.

Technologies Used

SolidWorks KeyShot Ergonomic Analysis User Research Material Selection

Project Details

This water bottle design project began with extensive user research to identify pain points in existing hydration products. The focus was on creating a bottle that would be comfortable to hold for extended periods while maintaining optimal functionality and aesthetic appeal.

The ergonomic form features carefully considered grip zones, balanced weight distribution, and a cap design that facilitates one-handed operation. Material selection prioritized both durability and sustainability, with consideration for recyclability and long-term use patterns.

The design process included iterative prototyping, user testing, and refinement based on feedback. Special attention was paid to manufacturing feasibility, ensuring the design could be produced efficiently while maintaining quality standards. The final design represents a balance of form, function, and environmental responsibility.
