BELL UH-1 IROQUOIS

Complex Systems Modelling & Digital Twin Engineering

Project Overview

A comprehensive 3D model and digital twin of the iconic Bell UH-1 Iroquois helicopter, focusing on accurate systems modeling and engineering detail. This project demonstrates advanced CAD modeling capabilities and understanding of complex mechanical systems.

Technologies Used

SolidWorks AutoCAD KeyShot 3D Modeling Systems Engineering

Project Details

The Bell UH-1 Iroquois, commonly known as the "Huey," is one of the most recognizable helicopters in aviation history. This project involved creating a detailed digital twin that captures both the external aesthetics and internal mechanical systems of this legendary aircraft.

The modeling process required extensive research into the helicopter's engineering specifications, including rotor dynamics, transmission systems, and airframe construction. Special attention was paid to maintaining historical accuracy while demonstrating modern CAD modeling techniques.

This project showcases skills in complex systems modeling, attention to detail, and the ability to work with intricate mechanical assemblies. The final model serves as both a technical demonstration and a tribute to this iconic piece of aviation history.
