Jacob Fletcher

Product Design Technology & Systems Engineer

I am a Product Designer and Systems Engineer driven by a fascination with how complex things work. From deconstructing the mechanics of a helicopter to engineering a device that replicates atmospheric phenomena, my passion lies in bridging the gap between ambitious ideas and functional, real-world technology.

My unique background combines the user-centric focus of Product Design Technology with a formal education in IT systems and a practical grounding in programming. This allows me to not only design a physical product but also to understand and contribute to the software and electronics that bring it to life. I thrive on deconstructing complex problems and building robust, elegant, and manufacturable solutions.

My Philosophy

From Concept to Code

I believe a great product is more than just its physical form. My approach integrates hardware design (CAD), software logic (Python), and electronics (Arduino) into a single, cohesive system.

Design for Reality

Advanced 3D modelling is my primary tool, but I always design with an eye toward manufacturing. I apply DFM principles for 3D printing, CNC machining, and injection moulding to ensure my concepts are not just beautiful, but buildable.

Inspired by Nature, Driven by Data

I leverage computational tools like Rhino Grasshopper to create optimised, generative designs inspired by natural systems. This data-driven approach allows for the creation of solutions that are both efficient and innovative.

Iterate and Validate

I embrace an iterative design process, moving from sketches and low-fidelity models to fully functional prototypes. Rigorous testing and validation are at the heart of how I ensure a solution is truly robust and reliable.

Systems Thinking

I apply systems thinking and data analysis to deconstruct complex problems and engineer effective, data-driven solutions that consider the entire product ecosystem.
