BLUETOOTH NETFLIX SPEAKER

Product Design & Brand-Driven Aesthetics

Project Overview

A retro-inspired Bluetooth speaker design that combines nostalgic aesthetics with modern technology. This project explores brand identity integration while maintaining functional excellence in consumer electronics design.

Technologies Used

Fusion 360 KeyShot Photoshop Product Design Brand Integration

Project Details

This Bluetooth speaker design merges the iconic Netflix brand aesthetic with retro consumer electronics styling. The project challenged me to create a product that would appeal to streaming enthusiasts while maintaining high-quality audio performance and modern connectivity features.

The design process involved extensive research into both vintage speaker aesthetics and contemporary audio technology. The final form factor balances portability with acoustic performance, featuring carefully designed speaker grilles and intuitive control interfaces.

Special attention was paid to brand integration, incorporating Netflix's signature red color scheme and typography in a way that feels authentic to the brand while creating a desirable consumer product. The speaker includes features like voice assistant integration, multi-device pairing, and streaming service connectivity.
